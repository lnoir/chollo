Frontend Developer - Leading Biomedical Saas solution!

Are you a skilled Frontend Developer looking to take your next step? Does the opportunity to work with cutting edge software with the Biomedical/Pharmaceutical sector excite you?

If so, our client has the perfect role for you. We are currently looking for a Frontend Developer with strong ReactJS experience to join a dynamic, growing and skilled team.

You will be working on improving user experience across the full product range. You will be working in the team to rapidly prototype and design new UI components. This role will be perfect for someone looking to make a mark for themselves as a front-end authority in company that will nurture skills with training and direct customer experience.

The ideal Frontend Developer will have a combination of the following skills and experiences:

  • A few years industry experience building out frontend applications using ReactJS and Javascript.
  • Experience working within a biomedical/pharmaceutical sector is not essential. If you have worked on cutting edge applications (ideally saas) you will be great for the role.
  • Ideally a computer science degree or equivalent.

Salary & Benefits:

  • £60k
  • Flexible Working Hours
  • Pension
  • Cycle scheme
  • Flexible Holiday allowance
  • Partial Remote Working Option (2 days a week on site)
  • Eye sight Subscription

If successful you will get the chance to take the React infrastructure to the next level and apply the benefit of your experience in improving UI standards and components.

Interviews will begin on the 23/01 so please make sure you send your resume to or apply via the link to avoid missing out!
