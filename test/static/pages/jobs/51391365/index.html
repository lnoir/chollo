Informatica Developer – 6-month Contract – Up to £550 a day plus uplifts – London

Key Skills: Informatica/SSIS, ETL Processes, Data warehouse architecture, SQL Server, PowerShell

An exciting opportunity to work with a big-name client within the finance sector. The client is looking for a highly motivated and eager individual with strong communication skills, to join their team.

The candidate will need a strong knowledge of software development with the role mainly focusing on working within the IT Development team, who are accountable for the design, development and maintenance of ETL solutions and exchange data warehouse systems.

Location: London, hybrid working with 2 days in the office, must be eligible to work in the UK and be able to come into the office if required.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Liaise with business analysts to understand complicated business specifications. Create application design and development specifications to align with these requirements.
  • Develop unit test specifications and operate methodical testing of the delivered software.
  • Developing Power BI data models and recommending best practice strategies for creating reports and visualizations 
  • Install and test software releases within the development environment
  • Be accountable for the accuracy of supplied estimates
  • If required, you will need to be able to provide support outside of allocated working hours
  • Adopt best practices around DevSecOps

Key Experience:

  • Previous experience with ETL Processes
  • Good level of experience using ETL tools e.g. Informatica/SSIS
  • Experience working with Data Warehouse concepts
  • Software development experience

Desirable Skills:

  • Experience writing UNIX/ PowerShell scripts
  • The ability to be a mentor for other
  • Is strong both individually and as part of a team
  • Strong analytical/problem solving skills
  • Flexible in terms of working times
